Lasagne

Homemade Lasagne With A Twist

A creamy golden delicious lasagne, made up of low fat Turkey Mince, a rich tomato sauce. We were inspired to create this lasagne as we searched for a low fat alternative to the traditional calorific lasagne we have all come to know and love.

We hope you will love the receipe as much as we do

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Chopped Tomatos
  • Carrots
  • Herbs
  • Stock
  • Turkey Mince
  • Tomato Puree
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Red Wine

Steps To Make The Dish

  1. Fry off your turkey mince and put to the side
  2. Chop the carrots, onion and any other veg
  3. Fry off the veg
  4. Add the turkey mince back into the pan
  5. Chop and introduce the garlic to the pan
  6. Once browned off, put your chopped tomatos and red wine into the pan and simmer
  7. Heat up the butter, add the flour until turned into a paste, add milk slowly whisking all the time, once mixed take off the heat and add cheese.
  8. Add a small layer of mince and veg to the bottom of a deep oven-proof dish and then layer with pasta sheets, cheese sauce then repeat. Once the dish is full, top with cheese
  9. Put the dish in the oven for 30 mins on 180 fan
  10. Finally top with fresh basil, parmesan and serve with garlic bread
